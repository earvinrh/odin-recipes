Cheeseburger

Description

Classic American dish known as the Cheeseburger.

Items

  • Hamburger Buns
  • Frozen Patty
  • American cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Thousand Island dressing

Steps

  1. Thaw out patty and place it on the griddle. Add salt and pepper.
  2. Cut up lettuce and tomato into thin slices.
  3. While patty is cooking, add cheese on top.
  4. Place both buns face on the griddle.
  5. Add thousand island dressing to the bun.
  6. Place patty on bottom of the bun.
  7. Add lettuce and tomato to the burger.
  8. Enjoy!
