Tortas

Description

This recipe is a traditionally used recipe amongst Mexican people.

Ingredients

  • Bolillo
  • Jamon
  • Queso
  • Hitomate
  • Lechuga
  • Chiles Jalapeno
  • Mayonesa

Steps

  1. Spread mayonesa on the bolilo buns and place on griddle.
  2. Place jamon on griddle.
  3. Place queso on top of jamon and then flip over.
  4. Place both jamon and queson on the crispy bolilo.
  5. Add your sliced hitomate, lechuga and chiles jalapeno on the bolilo.
  6. After torta is put together, place it back on the griddle to get a crispy finish then enjoy.
